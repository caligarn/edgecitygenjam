THE MATCH

One brief. Four hours. One film.

A GenJam is a live test of how fast you can think, write, and create under the clock.

  1. THE BRIEF

    You get one brief.

    Every team in the matchup gets the same prompt, revealed live.

  2. THE SPRINT

    Four hours on the clock.

    Come up with a story and build an AI film around it — live.

  3. THE FILM

    Made with your team.

    Think, write, generate, and cut together. Speed is part of the craft.

  4. THE VERDICT

    Same-day submit, same-day results.

    You make it and submit it in the session. No homework.
